JJ Lin

JJ Lin is a Singaporean singer, songwriter, record producer and actor. He recently held a concert at the National Stadium on the 4 and 5 November. The most recent award he recieved is the the 2018 Mnet Asian Music Awards as the Best Asian Artist.

Stefanie Sun

Stefanie Sun is a Singaporean singer-songwriter. In 2000, she released her debut album, Yan Zi, which won her a Golden Melody Award for Best New Artist. Having sold more than 30 million records, she achieved popularity in Greater China and among the Chinese-speaking world.

Songs:
  • 遇见
